Just Write!

We’ve gotten to the first official post and we’re at a roadblock. I just have too much to write about!

The problem that I’ve had time and time again with this blog is that I find myself being lost for words because I don’t know what to talk about first. I come up with idea after idea with what I can say and honestly, I just get too caught up in what should be the best and didn’t take a moment to just write. This is meant to be a personal log of my thoughts, emotions, and experiences in the field and throughout life. There’s no correct or incorrect way to portray thoughts unless they’re not the actual thoughts themselves.

The concept of this blog’s direction has moved from place to place and I’ve come to the understanding that this is something to nurture and to grow. It could be an expression of feelings of imperfection or injustice or, it could be advice on how I overcame hardships and the experiences around them. At the moment, the concept is that you (the reader) would have to either go on to my social media profile or scan my business card and then read the thoughts that have run across my mind. If you don’t like what I have to say that’s okay. But it shouldn’t be something to hold against my character or make an attempt to demean or “Make fun of” me because this may just not be meant for you and you don’t have to read on.

Furthermore, at the time of writing this, I’m currently balancing:

  • My career
  • A social Life
  • A university degree
  • General life things

This (as can be assumed) is quite a balancing act and this could mean that there’s (unintentionally) little consistency with posting but I’ll just try to make time where I can.

But enough of the complaining. At the moment I’m working on posts with the titles:

  • Greener Grasses
  • Imposter Syndrome
  • Progression
  • Pathways
  • Crossroads
  • Envy
  • Future

Currently, I’ve got some big ambitions and I hope that you follow my journey. Also, feel free to contact me either via email at “contact@taylorwatson.co.uk” (or you can click the email icon in the top right, or message me on LinkedIn because I’d love to answer any questions you have or to just have a chat.
